Dietary Restrictions

  • No pork (this included candies and other food products made with pork gelatin or enzymes)
  • Meat must be Halal. This means that the animal has to be slaughtered a certain way. Before the animal is killed a prayer is said; making it halal and permissable for muslims to consume.
  • We can consume anything from the land or sea. One thing that isn't allowed is animals who live in mud or under the surface.
  • Foods marked with the 'halal' symbol below are permissable

    • Why is alcohol prohibited in Islam

    • Muslims believe in taking care of the their health. Drinking alcohol harms your body in many ways so drinking is prohibited.
