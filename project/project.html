My Favorite Movies

10/15/21

By Allia Dalana


1) To come up with ideas for this project I thought about summer 2020. During that time I watched a lot of movies and I was trying to think back to that time to remember which ones I liked the most. I knew I wanted the background color to be red because this color reminds me of movie premieres and red carpets. I created my prototype on google slides because I felt that it would showcase my imgaination even more than on paper.

2) I learned how to insert links and images without looking back onto previous labs.

3) The hardest part was getting my banner to display.

4) To fix this issue, I created a duplicate of my banner and tried again. I also checked for any mistakes and typos.

5) The color scheme for each of the movie pages I created were based entirely on the color scheme of the movie poster.
